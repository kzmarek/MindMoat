Privacy Policy for MindMoat
Effective Date: March 25, 2026

MindMoat is designed to be a private, local-first productivity tool.

1. Data Collection and Transmission
MindMoat does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data processing occurs locally within the user's browser.

2. Data Usage
    - Browsing Activity: The extension monitors URLs of active tabs solely to compare them against the user-defined blocklist. This data is processed in-memory and is never logged or exported.

    - Extension Settings: User-defined blocklists and time quotas are stored using the chrome.storage.local API. This data remains on the user's device and is not accessible by the developer or any third parties.

3. Third Parties
No data is shared with third parties. There are no analytics, tracking scripts, or advertising modules integrated into MindMoat.

4. Open Source Transparency
As an open-source project, the full implementation of MindMoat's data handling can be audited at: https://github.com/kzmarek/MindMoat
